Park Minsook

Artist Park Min-sook expresses the formative meaning of the lines by creating a pattern of lines using the marquetry technique. Lines are not just elements of simple formative expression, but contain inner thoughts and emotions. The repetitive line patterns that stand out in the variation of shape and density reveal the accumulated time and condensed energy of scraping and filling one by one.
