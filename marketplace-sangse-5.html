Choi Soojin

Sujin Choi rubs or kneads minerals on a flat surface and then bakes them based on painting and pottery. I feel love and boredom for various things that exist in the world, such as industrial products, works of art, and artifacts. Imitating their iconography, I am interested in the institution and environment of the art world where materials exist as works and products. Currently, I am continuing my work at my personal studio ‘Material World’ in Yongsan-gu, Seoul.
